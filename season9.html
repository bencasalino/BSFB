To Join/Sign Up for next season:

1. Pay League Fee -

League Fee for Season 9 is $50.00.

Paypal OR mail accepted for payment.

Message a commissioner when ready.
Ask Rafa for his current address if mail is your payement method.

Protected Draft Pick Deadline: August 1st

Season 9 Sign-Up Deadline: September 1st

2. Accept Invite/Create Team -

Once payment is received, you will recieve a EPSN invite via your ESPN Account email.

ONLY the Email associated with your current "ESPN account" will work. If you do not have an ESPN account you will need to create one to join our league.

If the "invivitaion link" is not working please message a commissioner.

3. Choose Team Name -

After accepting the invite from the "Edit Team" tab you can update team details and choose a name.

Note: *'s in team name may only be used to denote championship(s).

4. Select a Team Logo -

All managers competeing in Season 9 will get a logo. Below are some templates I've designed; these do NOT have to be your team colors. Please message me with what logo you want and with colors and/or name of what you would like it to display. Logo selection is also baised on the sign up order.

5. Join FB Group -

Our league Facebook group is where the league talks about trade chat / banter / rules.

Managers are encouraged to join as all the most recent league updates will be posted on the group page.
